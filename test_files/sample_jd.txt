Senior Product Manager - Job Description

About the Role:
We are looking for a Senior Product Manager to lead our B2B SaaS platform.

Requirements:
- 5+ years of product management experience
- Experience with B2B SaaS products
- Strong analytical skills and data-driven decision making
- Excellent communication and stakeholder management
- Technical background or ability to work closely with engineering
- Experience with Agile/Scrum methodologies
- SQL proficiency for data analysis

Responsibilities:
- Define product vision and roadmap
- Work with engineering to deliver features
- Analyze user data and market trends
- Manage stakeholder expectations
- Drive product metrics and KPIs

Nice to have:
- MBA or equivalent
- Experience in fintech or enterprise software
